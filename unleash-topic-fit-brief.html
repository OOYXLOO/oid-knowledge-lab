Article outline

Draft sequence

  1. Why a green build is not the same as rollout readiness.
  2. What feature flags add to continuous delivery: exposure control, blast-radius reduction, and reversible decisions.
  3. Generate release evidence as JSON and Markdown.
  4. Render a small internal review page.
  5. Run a publish guard before enabling broader exposure.
  6. Use flag states to pause, roll forward, or roll back without emergency redeploys.
  7. Close with a checklist for enabling a feature flag safely.
