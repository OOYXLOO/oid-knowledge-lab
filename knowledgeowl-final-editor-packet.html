Copy-ready pitch

Email body

Hi KnowledgeOwl team,

I would like to pitch a paid guest post for the KnowledgeOwl blog.

Title:
How to Turn AI-Assisted Help Content Reviews into Evidence Logs

Summary:
AI-assisted drafting can help documentation and support teams move faster, but a polished draft is not the same as a reviewable draft. This article would show knowledge base owners, support leads, and documentation writers a lightweight evidence-log workflow for reviewing help content before it reaches customers.

Brief outline:
1. Why confident AI-assisted help drafts still need a visible review trail
2. Start with the reader question and intended audience
3. Separate source facts from draft claims
4. Capture reviewer checks and publication blockers
5. Add a short decision summary before publishing
6. Keep private support data, account screenshots, and copied third-party text out of the handoff

Why it fits KnowledgeOwl:
- It is written for documentation teams, knowledge-base owners, support leads, and technical writers.
- It focuses on documentarian quality of life: clearer review status, fewer risky claims, and easier editor handoff for non-programmer reviewers.
- The workflow can be used without adopting a new engineering tool.
- I am an independent writer, not an agency, and I am not asking for a required backlink or anchor text.
