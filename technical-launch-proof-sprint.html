Best fit

For prototypes that work, but do not explain themselves yet

Many technical demos are useful but hard to evaluate in the first minute. This sprint turns scattered notes into a review path: what the product does, why it matters, what proof exists, what is still missing, and what should not be claimed publicly yet.

Client intake

What to send before scoping

A good first request includes the public demo or repository link, the audience, the materials that already exist, the one-minute message reviewers should understand, and the claims that should not be made publicly yet. Remove credentials, private customer data, payment information, and unreleased confidential material before sharing anything.
