Generated media review workspace

Media Provenance Studio turns AI-generated assets into a reviewable delivery ledger.

Creative and technical teams can register generated images, clips, diagrams, and campaign assets with prompt summaries, model notes, storage references, license boundaries, reviewer status, and final fingerprints before sharing them with a client or editor.

OID Intelligence Lab AI evaluation packet Writing samples
1. Register

Capture the asset name, media type, storage URL, creator note, model family, and prompt summary.

2. Fingerprint

Record a stable hash or delivery reference for the approved file instead of relying on filenames.

3. Review

Mark each asset as draft, review, approved, rejected, or archived with a short boundary note.

4. Package

Export a compact handoff sheet with approved assets, unresolved risks, and source notes.

5. Reuse

Keep a clean asset ledger for future ads, docs, short videos, and product pages.
