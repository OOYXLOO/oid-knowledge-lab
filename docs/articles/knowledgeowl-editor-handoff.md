# KnowledgeOwl Editor Handoff

## Article Pitch

**How to Turn AI-Assisted Knowledge Base Updates into Evidence Logs**

This article is for documentation managers, support leads, technical writers, and knowledge base owners who want AI-assisted drafts to be easier to review before they reach customers.

## Why It Fits

The article focuses on documentarian quality of life rather than AI hype. It gives teams a small, repeatable handoff they can use inside normal tickets, pull requests, Slack threads, or editorial review notes:

1. Reader question and audience.
2. Source facts.
3. Draft claims.
4. Reviewer checks.
5. Publication blockers.
6. Privacy boundaries.
7. Publish, revise, hold, or reject decision.

## Publication Boundary

The final article should not include private customer data, credentials, account screenshots, copied third-party text, unpublished support conversations, payment information, or required promotional backlinks. Public examples use synthetic scenarios and focus on review workflow.
