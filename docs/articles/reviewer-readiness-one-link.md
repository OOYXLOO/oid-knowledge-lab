## Review Method

1. Define the expected behavior or reader outcome.
2. Identify failure modes, unsafe inputs, and missing evidence.
3. Write or describe focused checks before accepting the work.
4. Separate source facts from draft claims and guesses.
5. Give a clear verdict with evidence, suggested fix, and residual risk.
