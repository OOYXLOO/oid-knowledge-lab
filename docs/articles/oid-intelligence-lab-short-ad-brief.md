# OID Intelligence Lab Short Ad Brief

## Concept

OID Intelligence Lab helps teams inspect object identifier records before they reuse them in internal tools, AI workflows, or compliance reports.

The short ad frames the product around a simple risk: an AI system can sound confident while citing identifiers, namespaces, or registry records that nobody has verified. The product turns that uncertainty into a reviewable trail: source links, record snapshots, consistency checks, and a compact evidence page that can be shared with developers or reviewers.

## Audience

- Developers building AI agents that need trusted reference data.
- Security and compliance teams reviewing identifier use.
- Technical writers and solution architects explaining registry-backed workflows.

## 30-Second Story

1. A developer sees conflicting identifier references across docs, dashboards, and model output.
2. OID Intelligence Lab pulls the record into a structured review page with source links and status checks.
3. The reviewer sees conflicts, missing evidence, and verified fields before the data is reused.
4. The final line: `Trust the identifier before you trust the answer.`

## Visual Direction

- Start with scattered identifier strings and unresolved references.
- Transition into a clean evidence graph: identifier, source, status, change history, and reviewer note.
- End on the public product page and a single proof link.
