Source boundary

Publishable by design

The published site combines open IANA registry data with OID-base sitemap-level metadata. Raw copied third-party page bodies are excluded from the public repository unless a future source authorization explicitly allows them.

Technical boundary

Model output is not source truth

Qwen is positioned as the explanation layer after deterministic checks. The system keeps source facts, generated summaries, review decisions, and human approval gates separate.
