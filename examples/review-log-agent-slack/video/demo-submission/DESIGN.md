# Design Direction

## Style Prompt

Technical editorial demo with a quiet product-review tone: dark ink background, warm paper panels, precise mono data, and restrained motion. It should feel like a review room for source-aware documentation, not a sales landing page.

## Colors

- Background: `#111315`
- Panel: `#f3eee2`
- Ink: `#161718`
- Muted text: `#7c7568`
- Accent: `#d6a548`
- Success: `#4d8b6f`
- Risk: `#b85c4d`

## Typography

- Display and headings: `Fraunces`, fallback `Georgia`, serif
- Code and labels: `IBM Plex Mono`, fallback `Consolas`, monospace
- Body: `Aptos`, fallback `Arial`, sans-serif

## Motion

- Medium editorial energy.
- Primary transition: warm crossfade with a thin accent sweep.
- Every scene uses entrance animation.
- No rapid cuts, no neon, no gradient text.

## What Not To Do

- Do not show private Slack workspaces.
- Do not show account pages, tokens, cookies, customer data, or payment details.
- Do not mention revenue goals or prize strategy.
- Do not use generic blue/purple SaaS gradients.
