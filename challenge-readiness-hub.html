Boundary notes

What is live, simulated, or gated

AreaStatusReason
Static demosLiveThe pages are public, browser-readable, and require no login.
Generated reportsLiveReports and proof packets are generated from local examples and checked by repository scripts.
Slack app behaviorSimulated until workspace setupThe review workflow can be demonstrated without reading private Slack messages or storing workspace data.
Backblaze storage adapterGated until account credentials existThe public prototype avoids embedding provider keys or private bucket data.

Source hygiene

Reviewers can verify the public boundary

The linked demos are designed around public or sanitized artifacts. They do not require private inboxes, account-local browser storage, cookies, financial records, identity documents, private customer exports, or hidden platform pages.
