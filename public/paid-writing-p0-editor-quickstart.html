Review boundary

What these packets intentionally avoid

The samples do not include credentials, private exports, customer data, private screenshots, copied third-party page bodies, or production account details. Public proof pages and raw Markdown packets are included to make implementation depth reviewable without exposing private systems.

Editorial fit

Why these topics are grouped together

  1. Each article turns a messy review workflow into a repeatable handoff.
  2. Each topic is supported by public implementation artifacts instead of a loose pitch only.
  3. Each draft can be revised to match the publication's voice, depth, screenshot rules, and final format.
