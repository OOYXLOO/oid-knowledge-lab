Article fit

Proposed tutorial

The article shows how a small team can model a public registry source and a sanitized local inventory as separate inputs, then produce reviewable JSON, CSV, Markdown, and static dashboard artifacts. The Airbyte framing maps the public source to Connector Builder or a low-code source prototype, the local inventory to an approved file source, and the derived results to a destination or warehouse-backed review table.

Reader questionReview answer
Why Airbyte?The workflow is about moving data safely across source boundaries, preserving source context, and making downstream review artifacts reproducible.
What is built?A registry evidence dashboard with normalized rows, status classification, derived reports, a manifest, and a publish guard.
What stays out?Private customer inventories, credentials, account exports, payment data, contact-level registry exports, and copied third-party page bodies.
