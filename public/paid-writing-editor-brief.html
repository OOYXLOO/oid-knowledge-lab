Publication boundary

Fresh work, reviewable proof

These pages are review aids, not recycled submissions. Final articles can be revised for each publication's voice, length, and editorial standards. Public samples avoid private customer exports, account screenshots, credentials, support-thread bodies, copied third-party article text, and unpublished client code.
