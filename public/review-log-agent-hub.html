What this prototype proves

The working demo shows a concrete AI-era documentation workflow: capture a team discussion, separate source facts from draft claims, surface reviewer checks and publication blockers, then produce a handoff that an editor or support owner can review before publishing.

Workflow Conversation to evidence

Slack-style messages become a structured review log instead of a loose summary.

Quality Blockers stay visible

The handoff preserves publication blockers and reviewer checks instead of smoothing them away.

Safety Boundary-first output

The Markdown explicitly excludes passwords, API tokens, cookies, private tickets, payment data, and copied article bodies.

Markdown handoff sections

The prototype emits a readable handoff rather than a raw transcript. It is intentionally small enough to inspect, copy, and adapt.

Source FactsCaptures verified facts from docs, policy, or owner comments.Makes the factual basis reviewable.
