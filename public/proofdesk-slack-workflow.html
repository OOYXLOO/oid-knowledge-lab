Boundary

Public or sanitized inputs only

ProofDesk should not request or store credentials, tokens, cookies, payment information, identity documents, private customer inventories, private account exports, or raw third-party page bodies. The public repository only contains original code, sanitized examples, generated reports, and source-boundary notes.
