Pitch

Turn public claims into source-aware proof packets

ProofDesk accepts a public artifact URL or sanitized claim list, normalizes each claim, separates ready items from human-gated items, and generates Markdown plus JSON proof packets that can be shared in review threads.

Demo script

Six-step walkthrough

1Open the ProofDesk packet demo.
2Review the sample claim JSON.
3Add or edit a claim with a public artifact URL.
4Generate a proof packet in the browser.
5Copy Markdown or download JSON.
6Show how ready claims and human-gated claims stay separate.

Boundary

Public or sanitized inputs only

ProofDesk should not collect credentials, tokens, cookies, private exports, private customer data, payment data, identity records, or raw third-party page bodies. Final approval stays with a human reviewer.
