Publication boundary

What stays outside the public proof package

The public proof package avoids credentials, customer inventories, private account exports, copied page-body mirrors, unpublished client material, and source material that requires separate authorization. Final articles should be written fresh for the accepting publication using that publication's product details, screenshots, and editorial style.
