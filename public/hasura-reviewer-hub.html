Proposed tutorial

The article shows how a small team can turn a sanitized OID inventory into a GraphQL-backed review queue. The Hasura framing maps local evidence rows to Postgres tables, exposes filtered review queries, and keeps mutation permissions separate from public reporting.

Copy-ready pitch

Short application text

I would like to propose an implementation-backed tutorial: Build a GraphQL Review API for OID Inventory Cleanup with Hasura. The article would show how to model sanitized OID inventory rows in Postgres, expose reviewer queues through Hasura GraphQL, add role-aware review mutations, and publish safe Markdown/CSV evidence summaries without including credentials, private customer exports, or copied third-party page bodies.
