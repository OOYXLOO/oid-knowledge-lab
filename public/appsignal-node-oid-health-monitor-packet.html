Proposed outline

Article structure

  1. Explain why review queues need operational health signals.
  2. Model sanitized OID findings as a small Node.js dataset.
  3. Build a health snapshot with status and severity counts.
  4. Expose `/health` for readiness and review queue status.
  5. Expose `/metrics` for monitor-friendly gauges.
  6. Define alert thresholds and no-secret observability boundaries.
