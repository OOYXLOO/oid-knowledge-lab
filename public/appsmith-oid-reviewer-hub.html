Article fit

Build an internal review queue, not just a dashboard

The proposed tutorial would show how to turn a sanitized OID inventory export into an Appsmith app with a table, filters, review status controls, reviewer notes, and a copyable Markdown handoff. It uses OID Knowledge Lab as the implementation-backed sample without publishing raw private inventories or copied third-party page bodies.

Reader questionReview answer
Why Appsmith?The workflow needs internal-tool UI: data table, filters, status updates, safe notes, and exportable handoff text.
What is built?A small review app for invalid OID syntax, unknown enterprise numbers, policy OIDs, status decisions, and next-step notes.
What stays out?Credentials, account exports, customer inventories, payment data, raw logs, copied page bodies, and private platform messages.
