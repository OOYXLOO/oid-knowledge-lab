Article promise

Make AI-assisted support content reviewable

The proposed article shows how a documentation team can turn an AI-assisted draft into a reviewable knowledge base update: define the user question, record source boundaries, keep a short evidence log, run repeatable checks where possible, and mark what still needs human judgment.

NeedReview answer
PracticalityThe workflow is small enough for support and documentation teams to apply without a new platform migration.
AI boundaryThe article treats AI drafts as material to review, not as an authority to publish blindly.
ProofThe examples are backed by public OID Knowledge Lab artifacts, generated reports, and release guards.

Publication boundary

Fresh article, safe proof

This page is a review aid. A final KnowledgeOwl article should be freshly written for the editor's audience and should avoid private customer content, account-local screenshots, copied third-party article bodies, credentials, and payment data.
