Article promise

Make AI-assisted support content reviewable

The proposed article shows how a documentation team can turn an AI-assisted draft into a reviewable knowledge base update: define the user question, record source boundaries, keep a short evidence log, run repeatable checks where possible, and mark what still needs human judgment.
