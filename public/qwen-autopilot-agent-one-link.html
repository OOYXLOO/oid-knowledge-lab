Product angle

What the agent does

1Parse safe input

Accepts a sanitized CSV or pasted OID list. Raw customer exports, credentials, and private account pages stay outside the public workflow.

2Classify evidence

Compares OIDs against public IANA PEN data and OID-base sitemap metadata without copying OID-base page bodies.

3Draft next actions

Produces an evidence-ready handoff: invalid values, known enterprise roots, unresolved items, and suggested remediation steps.

4Ask before acting

Stops at human approval before contacting vendors, changing registries, opening tickets, or touching production systems.

Safety boundary

Why this is not a scraper dump

The public project publishes public IANA-derived data, OID-base sitemap metadata, generated summaries, and local browser audit tools. It does not publish copied OID-base page bodies, raw customer inventories, account exports, secrets, private messages, payment data, tax records, or identity documents.
