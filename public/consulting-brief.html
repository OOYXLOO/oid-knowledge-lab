Safety boundary

What stays out of the package

Private inventories, credentials, tokens, account exports, payment data, KYC material, private customer records, and copied OID-base page bodies are not published. The public repository contains tooling, sanitized examples, derived reports, sitemap-level metadata, and source-boundary receipts.
