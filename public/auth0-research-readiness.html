Feedback fit

Useful product-research angles

The strongest feedback areas are developer onboarding, first application setup, quickstart clarity, authorization model comprehension, production-readiness checks, and how AI-assisted tools should handle identity workflows without crossing credential or account-data boundaries.

Data boundary

Research-safe participation rules

This packet is designed for public pre-screening only. Feedback work should not include passwords, OTPs, cookies, API keys, tenant secrets, private account pages, payment data, tax or KYC details, unreleased research prompts, or copied third-party content.
