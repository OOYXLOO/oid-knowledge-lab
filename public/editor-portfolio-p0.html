Submission boundary

What these samples intentionally exclude

  • No account credentials, tokens, cookies, or API keys.
  • No private customer inventories, workspace exports, production logs, or account screenshots.
  • No payment, tax, KYC, bank, card, wallet, or private payout details.
  • No copied full third-party article bodies or private documentation mirrors.
  • No claim that a pitch has already been accepted by an editor.
