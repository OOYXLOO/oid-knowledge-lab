First-scope promise

What the starter review does

The first review answers a narrow question: which OID rows are valid, which have public evidence, which need internal owner confirmation, and which should be corrected before a broader cleanup starts.

PhaseActionOutput
1. IntakeReview a sanitized CSV, TSV, or one-OID-per-line sample.Input-shape receipt and safe-data boundary check.
2. ClassificationRun local/browser checks against known public PEN evidence and directory metadata.Invalid, evidence-ready, unresolved, and owner-review rows.
3. HandoffPackage derived findings without publishing raw client inventories.Markdown summary, remediation CSV, JSON evidence, and re-run checks.

Safe intake

What to send and what to remove

Send a sanitized sample with an oid column and safe labels such as device family, certificate profile, service, internal registry id, or MIB module. Remove credentials, tokens, customer names, production hostnames, billing data, private account exports, and any field that is not needed for OID classification.
